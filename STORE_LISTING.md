### Description
GudMoment helps you save and celebrate the special moments in your life — anniversaries, first dates, milestones, and memories that matter most.

**Track Your Moments**
Save any meaningful date and watch time unfold. GudMoment shows exactly how long it's been — from minutes to years — with a real-time, beautifully animated elapsed time display.

**Share With Loved Ones**
Generate a unique QR code for any moment and share it with your partner, friends, or family. They can scan it instantly to add the same moment to their collection. It's a fun way to stay connected through shared memories.

**Never Miss an Anniversary**
Set recurring reminders — yearly, every 6 months, monthly, or every 2 years — so you never forget to celebrate. GudMoment sends you a notification at 9 AM on the day.

**Customize Your Collection**
Personalize each moment with your favorite emoji and a custom color palette. Drag and drop to arrange moments in the order that feels right.

**Your Data, Your Way**
Export your moments as a backup and import them on any device. Everything is stored locally on your phone — no account needed, no cloud, no tracking.

**Beautiful Brutalist Design**
Bold typography, chunky borders, playful colors. GudMoment looks and feels different — in the best way.

### Full Description (4000 chars max)
GudMoment helps you save and celebrate the special moments in your life — anniversaries, first dates, milestones, and the memories that matter most to you.

Ever wondered exactly how long it's been since you first met someone special? Or how many days since that unforgettable trip? GudMoment tracks it all, beautifully.

★ TRACK YOUR MOMENTS
Save any meaningful date and watch time unfold in real-time. GudMoment shows exactly how long it's been — from minutes and hours to years, months, and days — with an adaptive, live-updating display.

★ SHARE WITH LOVED ONES
Generate a unique QR code ticket for any moment and share it with your partner, friends, or family. They can scan it instantly to add the same moment to their collection. A fun, personal way to stay connected through shared memories.

Set recurring anniversary reminders so you never forget to celebrate. Choose from yearly, every 6 months, monthly, or every 2 years. GudMoment sends you a notification at 9 AM on the day.

★ HOME SCREEN WIDGETS
Keep your moments front and center with two widget styles:
• Multi-Moment Widget — Display up to 5 moments with elapsed time
• Random Moment Widget — Rotates through your moments automatically

★ CUSTOMIZE YOUR COLLECTION
Personalize each moment with your favorite emoji and a custom color palette. Drag and drop to arrange moments in the order that matters to you.

★ YOUR DATA, YOUR WAY
Export your moments as a JSON backup and import them on any device. Everything is stored locally on your phone — no account required, no cloud, no tracking.

★ DARK MODE
Choose between light, dark, or system-default themes. GudMoment looks great in any lighting.

★ BOLD, PLAYFUL DESIGN
GudMoment features a distinctive brutalist design with bold typography, chunky borders, and vibrant colors. It looks and feels different — in the best way.
